Cooking Solo

Mechanics

  • The recipe book gives you instructions on how to cook a particular recipe
  • Each appliance has different setting and input slots to put in ingredients.
  • ingredients should be prepared from the field.
  • Each appliance would have a separate ui tab with a cooking mini-game
  • The mini-game mostly runs in the background, but would need occasional interaction.
  • Can make multiple of a recipe at once.

Dynamics

  • The player would need to shuffle and monitor the different tabs while serving customers.
  • The player can cook dishes in batches to avoid long wait times.

Aesthetic

  • Cooking solo should feel chaotic and very difficult to manage
