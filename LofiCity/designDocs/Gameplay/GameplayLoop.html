Main Gameplay Loops

Tertiary loop - Seasons and Year

  • The entire game takes place over one in-game year.
  • Each season (28 days), the player will be given certain goals to accomplish.
  • This will determine what the player focuses on in the smaller gameplay loops.
  • The various goals should encourage the player to interact with the different systems.

Secondary loop - Day

  • Each day, the player would need to decide what activities to focus on, based on current goals.
  • The player would have to base their actions around the schedule of NPCs.
  • The player should have enough time in a day to do various activities.
  • Seasonal changes and particular events can add extra variation to this.

Primary Loops - Main gameplay systems

  • The main gameplay systems consist of the following:
    • Restoring the field and setting up machines.
    • Farming, Fishing, Ranching and Foraging.
    • Cooking and selling food using a street vending cart.
    • Interacting with NPCs.
