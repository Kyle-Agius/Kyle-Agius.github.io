Reputation System

This system keeps track of the player’s reputation with different groups and individuals.

Mechanics

  • Each Named NPC and District has a score.
  • Each score is stored as an integer between -100 to 100, with zero being neutral.
  • Different actions increase or decrease the score, with the amount based on the difficulty and impact of the action.
  • Different scores can be linked together. A change of x in one score gives a change of xy in another, where y is between 0 and 1.
  • Strong benefits and consequences should be attached to each score.
  • The score should not be shown to the player.

Dynamics

  • Taking socially responsible choices typically lowers initial benefits but gives better long term rewards
  • Having limits to the score means it is possible to change both ways, though it could be difficult.
  • Linked scores can be used to show family dynamics.
  • Players would consider the social impact of each decision.

Aesthetic

  • The player should feel that their choices have more of an impact.
  • By not showing the score, the player should not treat social responsibility mathematically.
