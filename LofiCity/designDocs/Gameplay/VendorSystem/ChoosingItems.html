Choosing Items

This describes how the player sets the items to sell, and how customers choose what to buy.

Mechanics

  • The player has limited slots to set the recipes that can be sold.
  • Each customer will calculate a score for each option, based on the item attributes they like.
  • The customer will try to buy the highest ranked dishes that can satisfy their hunger score:
    • E.g. If their hunger is 5, they could get the best ranked Hunger-3 and Hunger-2 dish.
  • Food can only be sold to the customer if it is in the carts inventory.
  • The more that a customer scores a dish, the higher the base reputation earned.

Dynamics

  • The player would need to prioritise the recipes that are in the highest demand for that district.
  • The player can sell more during the time when customers are hungry.
