Customer Behaviour

Any NPC that goes to the cart becomes a customer. This determines what they are interested in and how they respond to different actions.

Mechanics

  • Each customer has varying characteristics:
    • Patience: How long they are willing to wait, and how long the interaction can be.
    • Hunger: the type of dishes they will order (e.g. light snack). Varies with the time of day.
    • Likes and dislikes: A list of item attributes that changes what they buy.
    • Interest: Items they can trade / services they can offer.
  • The customer would also have set states, such as nervous, tired, confident ...
  • The state should show in the character portraits.
  • Each customer would also have an underlying goal, which the player could help them reach through interactions.

Dynamics

  • Likes, dislikes, goals, interest and hunger can be planned around based on location and time.
  • The player will need to be able to quickly figure out the customer's traits and goal.

Aesthetics

  • Different characteristics should avoid selling items being repetitive.
  • As customers get impatient when waiting, the player is incentivised to serve customers quickly.
