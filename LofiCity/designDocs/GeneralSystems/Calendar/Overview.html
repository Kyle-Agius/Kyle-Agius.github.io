Calendar

Mechanics

  • A year is split into four seasons of 28 days each - 112 days in total.
  • The game takes place over one year.
  • Each day should be long enough for the player to do different activities (approx. 15 IRL minutes).
  • The speed that time passes at can be adjusted for certain activities, without the player noticing.
  • The world should react to the current day and season.
  • Certain festivals take place on specific dates.
  • The player will be returned to their house at the end of the day.
  • Some activities will take place between days.

Dynamics

  • Each day can act as a short play session, giving players a natural stopping point.
  • The player will need to manage their time wisely.
