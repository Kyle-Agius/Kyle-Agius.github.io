Dialogue

This refers to the dialogue with Named NPCs

Mechanics

  • Split into quotes and conversations
  • Quotes:
    • Short dialogue snippets for when the player interacts with an NPC.
    • The quote chosen should depend on the situation, and the character's relation.
    • These are also used in the cart.
    • Each quote should show the character's personality.
  • Conversations:
    • Longer sections of dialogue with more back and forth.
    • Can be accessed when the NPC is doing certain activities (eg sitting on a bench).
    • Each NPC has a list of conversation topics, which unlock based on the relation.
    • Conversations should focus on deeper topics, particularly at a high relation.
    • The player can have options for dialogue, but no option should be treated as wrong.
  • Listening to any dialogue should increase the relation level.

Dynamics

  • Dialogue encourages the player to interact with the NPCs they see, but not to seek them out specifically.

Aesthetics

  • Quotes and starting conversations should help introduce the NPC to the player.
  • Deeper conversations should feel more intimate, showing the increasing relation.
  • Conversations should feel more natural and unscripted than cutscenes.
  • Quotes should help the NPCs feel more alive.
