NPC Types

There are two distinct types of NPCs:

  • Named NPCs: These are specific characters, often with quest lines and dialogue.

    • Have their own schedule of activities and things they like.

  • Random NPCs: These NPCs are created to populate scenes. Various factors are used for variation:

    • Personality: Their general archetype (e.g. Nomad, gym-goer).
    • Interest: What job/field do they have experience in (e.g. Farming, Fishing)
    • Mood: Their current status. Can change after cart interaction.

Both types can interact with the cart.
