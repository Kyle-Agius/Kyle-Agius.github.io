Game Overview

A single-player resource management game. The player joins a co-operative trying to restore a degraded field. It should have a cosy atmosphere, but allow for thought provoking themes and stories. The game should be 20-30 hours long, with a linear story and ending.

Main Gameplay Systems

  • Gameplay Loops
  • Fieldwork - Restoring diverse ecosystems to the field and collecting produce.
  • Cooking - Discover recipes and cook the food.
  • Vending - Interact with NPCs and sell food using a street vending cart.

World Systems

  • NPCs - Named and Random NPCs with dynamic schedules and reputation.
  • Calendar System
