Main Themes

These themes should be explored in both gameplay and story. Players that are interested in these subjects should find a nuanced and thought provoking discussion. Meanwhile, the discussion of these should not get in the way of player's enjoyment.

Solarpunk

This is a relatively young movement and genre of fiction. It presents a more hopeful future, with technology and nature in harmony. Some of the main characteristics are:

  • Presents a hopeful image for what can be achieved in the future.
  • A focus on sustainability and renewable energy.
  • Aesthetics based on Art Nouveau and Studio Ghibli, with an abundance of plants.
  • Anti corporate capitalism, favouring collectivism. Open to different implementations.

Other Themes

  • Problems of excess consumerism and materialism.
  • Work life balance and self-care.
  • Multiculturalism, celebrating diversity.
  • Altruism and helping a community over profit.
